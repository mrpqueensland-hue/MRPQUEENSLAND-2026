Why MRPs Matter – Don't Let Hospitals Go Back to the 1950s

What Are Medical Radiation Professionals?

  • Radiographers: X-rays, CT, MRI – every diagnosis starts here.
  • Sonographers: Ultrasound for pregnancies, hearts, more.
  • Nuclear Medicine: Cancer detection & treatment tracers.
  • Radiation Therapists: Precise cancer radiation delivery.

~80% of hospital patients need MRP services. This isn't optional – it's modern medicine.
